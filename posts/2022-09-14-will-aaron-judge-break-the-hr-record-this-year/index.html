Will Aaron Judge break the HR record this year?

Looking at home runs per game played in some of the best seasons.

andrewstiegler
2022-09-14

Aaron Judge has been on a tear recently, hitting two more HR last night to get very close to the single-season record. Heres a look at Judges homeruns against games played this season.

It looks like a fairly linear slope, so Ill add a linear trendline and check the fit. Ill also add Bondss record-breaking season to the plot.

The linear regression has a correleation coefficient of about 0.99, so its a pretty good fit. Based on the linear model, Judge will end up with ~68.9 home runs for the year. Ill also add a few of the other top home run seasons for comparison.

Name Home Runs Year
Barry Bonds 73 2001
Mark McGwire 70 1998
Sammy Sosa 68 1998
